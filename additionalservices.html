ADDITIONAL SERVICES

______

We provide a wide range of additional services designed to complement our overall commercial interior design, fit out and refurbishment services for businesses throughout the UK. Please see below our additional services and we encourage you to contact us to find out more. If you don’t see the service you require on this page then please get in touch to discuss your requirements. It’s very likely we can still help you with what you need.

Our Commercial Services

Acoustics

We can supply office acoustic solutions including wall tiles, suspended ceiling tiles with balanced acoustics, ceiling grid tiles which fit directly into standard ceiling grid systems, vertical hanging panels and suspended acoustic panels all utilising noise absorbing materials. We have the solutions to reduce noise in the workplace.

Air Conditioning

From simple small areas needing heating and cooling to full mechanical design and installations, we provide energy efficient solutions.

Alarm Systems and Alarms

Remote control, keycode operational alarms are just a few ideas to keep the workplace safe and secure. We advise and install many systems.

Carpentry and Joinery

Our bespoke designs, joinery and carpentry department allows for anything to be made, from reception desks to café counters.

Commercial Electrics

From adding and moving floorboxes to completely new electrical installations, all building works carried out by Stirling Interiors will be NICEIC certified.

Decoration

From paint to stunning wall vinyl finishes, polished plaster or polished concrete, we specialise in creating the perfect finish for your workspace interior

Demolition

Need to remove walls or embarking on a complete rip-out? We are reliable, safe and experienced in all aspects of demolition.

Dry Lining and Plastering

Are you looking for areas to be replastered or new walls dry lined? Contact the team at Stirling Interiors for an efficient, high quality and expert service.

Flooring

Whether you’re looking to install non-slip flooring, vinyl, carpet tiles, ceramic tiling or wood, the Stirling team can provide a range of options to improve your flooring.

General and Emergency Lighting

We provide turnkey solutions for electrical refurbishment, including atmosphere-enhancing and emergency lighting, all of which meets rigorous safety standards.

Glazing

Whether you’re after glass partitions, meeting rooms, offices or feature areas, we have solutions for glazing in your commercial workspace.

Manifestation

Safety filming on glazed areas can be designed even with your logo or branding. Contact us for creative ideas for your workplace.

Mechanical and Electrical

We provide top quality mechanical and electrical designs and installations on all our projects and work to rigorous health and safety standards.

Mezzanine Floors

Whether you need to maximise space that is not being used at a high level, or even create another floor, contact us for designs and installations.

Moveable and Folding Walls

We offer a comprehensive range of sliding and folding wall systems that can create a more versatile workspace. Contact us today to find out more.

Partitions and Screens

If you need extra meeting rooms or office divisions we have years of experience in office partitions. Contact us to find out how we can help you.

Plumbing

From small plumbing repairs to full mechanical and electrical designs and installations, we can give you the help you need.

Power and Data

Need extra power and data integrated into desks, tables or work surfaces? From one off installs to full workplace solutions, we can help.

Shelving and Racking

High quality storage solutions such as racking and shelving will ensure your property stays tidy and organised, which will in turn streamline your operations.

Signage

Your logo says a lot about your business. We design, create and install signage, including company signs, corporate logos and glass manifestation.

Storage

We provide a wide range of discreet, attractive, ‘off-the-shelf’ and bespoke storage solutions, which can help you organise your workload and optimise your operations.

Suspended Ceilings

Suspended or acoustic, floating ceilings combined with creative lighting will enhance any interior. Our designs can help take your interiors to the next level.

Tiling

Tiling for floors, ceilings or walls should be both decorative and practical. We offer services from small areas to complete project refurbishments.

Whiterock

We can install whiterock, which is a versatile, attractive and hygienic wall cladding that is ideal for walls that need to be kept clean.
